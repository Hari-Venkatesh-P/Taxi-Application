A Taxi Booking application,in which the user is alloted with a taxi for his travel,when it is available.
When a taxi completes it's travel during the booking of taxi by another customer , then the current taxi which was in service is given more preference than the other available Taxi.
There are five stoppings in total , each at a distance of 10kms frm others.
The booking pay is 100 for first 10 kms then 40 for each 50 kms.
